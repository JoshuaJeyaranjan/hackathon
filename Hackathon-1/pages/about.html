About the creators

In the hallowed halls of BrainStation, Josh and Trishane, two software whizzes, were stirred by a mandatory Hackathon. This year's theme: Anything Goes!. Excitement ignited as they concocted "Poké Stadium," a virtual arena filled with whimsical Pokémon encounters. But, a dark cloud loomed. Louis, a mischievous hacker, aimed to thwart their dreams.

Undeterred, they toiled from 9:30 to 3:30, transforming the back room of the class into a coding sanctuary. The project grew, infused with humor and unique completely functional not broken features. Louis tried to sabotage our heroes but thankfully fell short.

At the Hackathon's finale, Poké Stadium reigned supreme, a testament to Josh and Trishane's teamwork. They partied in celebration, leaving Louis to ponder a valuable lesson in the joy of fair play.
